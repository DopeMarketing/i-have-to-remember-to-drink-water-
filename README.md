# I have to remember to drink water. 

## Overview
A mobile hydration tracking app that sends personalized reminders throughout the day and logs water intake to help users maintain optimal hydration levels.

## Problem
People forget to drink water regularly throughout the day, leading to dehydration and associated health issues like fatigue, headaches, and poor concentration

## Target User
Health-conscious individuals who struggle to remember to drink enough water during their daily routine

## Features
- Customizable water intake reminders
- Water consumption logging and tracking
- Daily hydration goal setting based on user profile
- Visual progress dashboard with charts and statistics
- Achievement badges and streak tracking
